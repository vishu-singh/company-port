What are the entry requirements?

Our rules are not completely rigid, and we do judge each application on its own merits. As a guide we typically look for the following:

Work experience minimum of two year's work experience. If you have less than this, you will need to show superior academic credentials or evidence of outstanding performance

Educational background you might have an undergraduate degree or equivalent qualification. If you do not, that is absolutely fine, we will look at your work experience and/or anything interesting you have achieved

A minimum of 25 contacts on LinkedIn this is for security purposes

Please sign up bellow to be added to our waitlist. Once we're ready for you we'll invite you to apply by filling in an online application form. We may conduct a telephone interview with you next before letting you know the outcome of your application.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ultimately, our goal is to transform the way careers education is approached at school by grounding it in the broad and rich firsthand experience of real professionals. In other words, we want a careers education deeply connected to reality, as well, of course, as being available to all students regardless of background, ability or location. The real value of joining ProPair as a volunteer Advisor is to be part of this mission and to that end, we only want to bring great people on board who align with this.

How It Works

1. Review your requests

We'll send you student requests that best fit your experience and expertise.

2. Select the requests you want to help with

Flexibility and choice is key for our Advisors and so you decide which requests you want to help with.

3. Make an impact

Your career advice can make a real difference to a young person's future and potentially hundreds of others too.
